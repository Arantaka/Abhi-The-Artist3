About ABHILASH

Contemporary Artist & Muralist

Born in Chalissery, Palakkad district, ABHILASH discovered his passion for art at a young age. His formal education in art began at the Guruvayur Institute, where he graduated with first-class honours in National Diploma in Mural Painting.

With over two decades of experience, ABHILASH has mastered a diverse range of artistic styles and techniques. His portfolio spans mural painting, modern art, texture art, abstract art, graffiti, minimalist art, 3D, and cement relief wall art.

ABHILASH's work has been featured in numerous exhibitions across India, and his murals can be found in prestigious locations in multiple countries. Currently based in Dubai, he continues to create commissioned works for private collectors and public spaces.

Philosophy

Artistic Vision

"My art is a conversation between tradition and innovation. I draw from Kerala's rich cultural heritage while embracing contemporary global influences. Every stroke and texture is an expression of both reverence for the past and excitement for the future. I believe art should evoke emotion, tell stories, and transform spaces."

"Art is my language to communicate with the world, bridging tradition and innovation through colors and textures."

— ABHILASH
