Mood Boards

Visual blueprints that guide the creative process

These mood boards serve as visual blueprints for my creative process, where Kerala's rich cultural heritage meets contemporary minimalism. Each image is a thoughtful synthesis of textures, colors, and motifs that narrate a story—bridging the past and the future.

The juxtaposition of elements reflects my signature style: abstract expressionism intertwined with cultural storytelling. Earthy tones like ochre and terracotta anchor the compositions, while modern accents introduce a contemporary edge. These mood boards are strategic frameworks for crafting art that is both timeless and boldly original.
