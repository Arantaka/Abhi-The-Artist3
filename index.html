Artistic Journey

From Kerala to Dubai

Born in Chalissery, Palakkad district, ABHILASH discovered his passion for art at a young age. Graduating with first-class honours in National Diploma in Mural Painting at Guruvayur Institute marked the beginning of his artistic journey.

With over 20 years of experience, he has mastered various art forms including mural painting, modern art, texture art, abstract art, graffiti, minimalist art, 3D, and cement relief wall art. His work has been featured in numerous exhibitions across India.
